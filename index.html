welcome to manav sevakendra

Every one of the three poor people in the world is found in India also 190.7 Million people are undernourished, within 20 years there will be dangerous contamination of 60% of the water sources, air pollution caused 6% total disease burden, 65% of the land is degraded in some way, 30% of gross agricultural output is lost due to soil degradation. Only 0.5% in rural India enjoys basic sanitation facility and on the other side there are 31 lakh NGO have been opened in India, India ranks 7th in world giving Index. India is witnessing an NGO boom; there is one for every 600 people. 15 lakh schools have been opened and there is an increasing investment in the health sector, 3598 hospital have been opened for better treatments.

These are two exact opposite types of scenarios found existing together. There is a gap between the givers and the receivers. There is a scarcity and there is a response towards it but both are not meeting because of a few reasons such as trust factor between the parties involved, no correct availability of the information, corruption etc.

This is where our role comes in. We bridge the gap between the need and the resources; we understand the demand and hence make the supply flow. Our NGO has only one motive, to reach the right person at the right time. After a team working tirelessly we have found out a way to reach the masses efficiently and that is through COMMUNITY LEVEL EMPOWERMENT . With local community service, one can understand the problem in detail and target it with not only financial but also non-financial recourses.

Everybody can be great because everybody can serve

Clean drinking water

India has 63 Million people who do not have access to clean water sources. With initiatives like rain water harvesting, fountain, wells system etc. one can adopt a particular area, form a community and install these system for a safer tomorrow.

CLEAN COOKING

Close to 1.1 Billion people lack access to electricity and 300 million out of these belong to India alone. 800 Million People in India still rely on dunk cakes and wood for meeting their cooking needs. This is not only harmful for the environment but also for their respiratory system. Education and Rural electrification plays a major role here.

MEDICAL AND HEALTH ISSUES

India health care system ranks 112 out of 190 countries according to World Health Organization. With an investment of 4.2% of the GDP in health good and services, there are still people deprived of basic health care. One of the solution for this is the community of doctors coming together to help and educate people for their basic health issues.

RURAL EDUCATION AND INNOVATIVE SUPPORT

36% of people from rural India are illiterate and only 5.4% have crossed secondary school stage. This can be eradicated by the community of teachers coming forward not only to teach in the best of colleges but also educate these children who are the future of the country.

ENVIORNMENTAL HAZARDAND CONTAMINATION

Almost 8%-9% total disease burden in India is attributed to air pollution. With people of major cities coming together to take a pledge to reduce pollution, use public transport and reduce the usage of machinery such as AC’s, refrigerators etc. which cause green house effect by releasing CFC’s.
